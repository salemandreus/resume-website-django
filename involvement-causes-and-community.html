Contributing To Local Causes

  • I feel it is important to support the community as a developer, to help bridge the gap experienced by many people not privileged to be afforded the same access to opportunity - with WhereIsMyTransport I've volunteered developer support through our Public Transport API at a hackathon co-hosted with Women In Tech in 2017.
  • In 2016/2017 I volunteered in several events as a code coach, speaker, and mentor at Code4CT, an organisation that teaches underprivileged girls to code, both on behalf of WhereIsMyTransport and also later in my personal capacity, as I have a strong passion for increasing diversity in the software, tech, and games industries.
  • I was also invited to speak on a panel at Life Choices, an event aimed more generally at helping people in other industries transition into software careers.
  • When I had the time I was involved in Tech-for-Good and Coding Soup, a biweekly initiative by the non-profit Codebridge centred around making community data more widely available to the public. I also became involved in ColorCode - a biweekly meetup that encourages collaboration, supporting new developers and those keen to pivot in their coding careers.
